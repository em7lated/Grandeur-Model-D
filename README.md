# Grandeur Model D
Grandeur Model D is a piano soundfont based on a Steinway Model D from Grandeur's library. Bright and clean quality samples.
Credits to GD CRAFT for the samples, I just converted his own original soundfont into the SFZ format with a bit of audio editing by me.
48000HZ sample rate, Lossless FLAC format, 24bit float, 10 presets to choose from.
To download, simply just go to Releases and click Download ZIP
